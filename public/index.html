FAQ

Common questions

Do your games collect any data?
Nope. Game progress lives on your device. We have no server, no analytics, nothing.
Do I need internet to play?
No. Everything runs offline.
How do I reset my progress?
Android: Settings → Apps → Game → Clear Data. iOS: delete and reinstall.
What devices are supported?
iOS 14+ and Android 6.0+, phones and tablets.
