Privacy Policy — Simple Spider

Last updated: May 30, 2026
App: Simple Spider  |  Package: simple_games.solitaire_spider

Summary

Simple Spider is an offline card game. It does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not send your personal data to any server. Game statistics are stored only on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app stores the following locally on your device:

DataPurpose
Best move count (fewest moves to win)Personal record
Top 10 scores (score, moves, elapsed time)Local leaderboard

This data is not linked to an identity and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or login
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs
  • No crash reporting services
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or location access
  • No payment data (the app is free with no in-app purchases)

Network Access

Simple Spider works entirely offline. The Android release build does not request the INTERNET permission. Debug builds may include it for development tools only.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data. We do not integrate any third-party services that collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

The game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on your device until you:

  • Clear the app's data in your device settings, or
  • Uninstall the app

Either action permanently removes all stored game data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any revisions. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
